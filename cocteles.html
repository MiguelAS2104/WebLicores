Coctelería de Autor

Discover a curated selection of signature cocktails, crafted to elevate the unique profiles of our artisanal Maceration Gold spirits.

Piña Colada Artesanal

Maceration Gold Tropical Blend

  • 2 oz Maceration Gold Tropical Blend
  • 2 oz Fresh Pineapple Juice
  • 1.5 oz Coconut Cream
  • Grated Nutmeg (Garnish)

Blend all ingredients with crushed ice until smooth and creamy. Serve in a chilled tumbler and garnish with freshly grated nutmeg and a pineapple leaf.

Crema Moka

Maceration Gold Cacao Reserve

  • 1.5 oz Maceration Gold Cacao Reserve
  • 1 oz Espresso (Cold)
  • 0.5 oz Coffee Liqueur
  • Dash of Heavy Cream

Shake vigorously with ice to build the crema. Strain into a chilled martini glass and garnish with three coffee beans for health, wealth, and happiness.

Frozen de Manzana Verde

Maceration Gold Clear Orchard

  • 2 oz Maceration Clear Orchard
  • 1 oz Sour Apple Schnapps
  • 0.5 oz Lime Juice
  • Ice

Blend ingredients with a generous amount of ice until a slushy consistency is achieved. Serve immediately garnished with an apple fan.

Soda Tropical de Calalá

Maceration Gold Passion Reserve

  • 1.5 oz Maceration Passion Reserve
  • 0.5 oz Passion Fruit Puree
  • Club Soda to top
  • Mint Sprig

Build in a highball glass over fresh ice. Stir gently to incorporate the puree, top with soda water, and garnish with fresh mint.
